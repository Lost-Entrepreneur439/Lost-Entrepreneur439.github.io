Support My Android Device Maintenance

I maintain multiple Android devices and ROM builds in my free time. Donations are used to purchase test devices, replacement parts, and tools required to keep these builds alive.

Why I only accept cryptocurrency

I currently do not have access to traditional banking or payment processors. Cryptocurrency allows me to receive donations without requiring a bank account, and convert them directly into the hardware and parts needed for development.

You do not need to donate crypto if you are uncomfortable with it -- this option is simply available for those who already use it.
